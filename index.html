Privacy Policy

Summary

SciSpark does not collect analytics, does not use advertising, and does not integrate third‑party SDKs. We do not transmit any personal data off your device.

Data We Store (On Device Only)

  • Optional child name (if you choose to enter one)
  • Selected age band and daily progress (streaks, quiz completion & score)
  • Favorites, earned badges, and your archive of completed facts
  • Notification preference and reminder time

This information is stored locally on your device using Apple’s UserDefaults and is not shared with us or third parties.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. Notification settings can be changed in the app or in iOS Settings.

Sharing

If you choose to export or share content (e.g., a PDF of today’s fact), iOS presents the system share sheet. SciSpark does not track or intercept what you share.

Children’s Privacy

SciSpark is designed for children with parental involvement. External actions (such as sharing) may require passing a parental gate to continue.

Contact

Changes to This Policy
